Environmental and Water Resources Engineering

The Environmental and Water Resources Engineering was established in 2017 in University Teaching Department, CSVTU, Newai Campus which offers 2-year M. Tech Degree Programme. This discipline helps to give a solution that comes out in the Water Resources field for their Management and Planning. The main aim of this department is to train the students to face various challenges and to find out their solutions in the field of Environmental and Water Resources Engineering. This discipline takes broad scientific area for technical advance to address the local and worldwide challenges that come with Environment and Water Resources.

Since 2017 department has emerged as leader in capacity building, research and consultancy services in various fields of Hydroinformatics and Geospatial technology to provide support in various engineering applications for the benefit of the Government departments and the Private agencies.
